## Executive Summary

This document identifies strategic acquirers for the 57-patent AI Vision portfolio, organized by industry vertical. Unlike typical M&A where value derives from EBITDA or talent, **this portfolio's primary value is defensive and offensive IP leverage** across high-growth markets.

**Portfolio Valuation Range:** $30M - $100M+

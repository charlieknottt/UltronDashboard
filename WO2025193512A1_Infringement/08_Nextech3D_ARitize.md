# Nextech3D.ai (ARitize3D)
## Potential Infringement of WO/2025/193512A1

**Product:** ARitize3D Platform
**Status:** Publicly traded, Amazon-certified 3D content provider, serving Kohl's, CB2, Pier 1

---

## How It Works

ARitize3D is an AI-powered platform that generates hundreds of photorealistic 3D assets from 2D product images. Certified to publish directly to Amazon Seller Central and Google Merchant Center.

**Pipeline:**
1. Input: 2D product images
2. AI-driven patented 3D technology processes images
3. Photorealistic 3D assets generated at scale
4. Optimized for real-time interaction and fast loading
5. Published to Amazon, Google, Shopify
